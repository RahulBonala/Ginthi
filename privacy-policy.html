Last updated: April 9, 2026

Gintic Technologies Private Limited ("Gintic", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website gintic.ai and use our services.

Personal Information: When you book a demo, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name and job title

Automatically Collected Information: When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages viewed.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

3. Data Processing and Security

Gintic is ISO 27001 certified and SOC 2 Type II audited. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

For our enterprise clients: your financial data is never used to train external AI models. We offer on-premise and private cloud deployment options where data never leaves your environment.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

5. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws and regulations.
